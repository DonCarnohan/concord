Welcome to Concord!

Concord is a place for people to organize meetings.

New Group

Discord Integration

Login using discord for convenience, including locking group members to those who have access to your server. There is also an optional bot which will announce when a meeting is posted and provide the basic information in the designated discord channel.

Groups

Organize groups of people who need to schedule meetups. Designed for gaming, but expandable to many applications. Use Concord to schedule your Dungeon and Dragons meetups, game training sessions, or team meetups.

In Development

Concord is open to feature requests (link to come). If your group has a specific need it may be added to the website provided we think it would be useful to a broader audience as well.
